Our Payment Provider

All payment information labeled {% include "payments/stripe_bage.html" %} is securely stored with our payment provider, Stripe. Stripe is fully compliant with industry security standards (certified PCI Level 1 Service Provider) and used by companies and nonprofits alike (e.g. Unicef, Booking.com, Indiegogo). We do not have a copy of your payment method (e.g. credit card number, card expiry date, CVV or bank account details) or payment history (i.e. past invoices) on our server.
