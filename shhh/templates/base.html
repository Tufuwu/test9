Keep secrets out of emails and chat logs.

Secrets are stored using advanced encryption and are only available for a limited amount of time. Shhh doesn't store your passphrases, they are only used to encrypt your secrets. Encryption method used: Fernet with password, random salt value and strong iteration count (100 000).

The project is open-source (MIT) and available here , so feel free to contribute, star, fork, spread love! There is everything you need to host this app on your own private / local server if you want even more security and privacy.
